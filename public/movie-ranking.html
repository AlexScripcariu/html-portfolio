My top 5 movies and series of all time

Home Alone 2

I love the New York atmosphere, while the moral teachings weren't as apparent as they were in the original film, the sequel had much funnier 'kevin' moments and overall it was a joy to watch.

Home Alone

This movie was great, describing the life of a shunned-down 10 year old. The climax of the movie is iconic today and loved by many fans, however I think it was somewhat short. Regardless it does outweigh the other parts of the movie. There were a lot of moral teachings on family relationships and kindness, so overall great film for all audiences.

The Big Bang Theory

As an enjoyer of mathematics and physics, this show is perfect for me. I rarely enjoy romcoms and other genres and as this was a comedy (my faovurite), this show is perfect for me. It combines my geek side of my brain with a more social. A fun mix of both worlds.

Brooklyn 99

Once again another comedy, but this time with a crime genre. I do like the element of mystery in these shows as it does 'tickle my fancy' yet this show did it well. Mind you, this wasn't as 'academic' as TBBT but in terms of the laughs and jokes produced, it definitely has a high mark.

Monk

As previously mentioned (or now mentioning), I love mystery shows as they also get me to question the cases and they are fun overall. Now, monk was an older TV series yet it had a much greater detectiveness compared to B99. While B99 was more comedy based, Monk was more intensity based.
